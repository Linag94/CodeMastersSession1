Welcome to AmberClan, the 5th clan.

AmberClan is a clan that was shaped during the series: "dawn of the clans".

Amberclan's leader, Amberstar, is a noble leader, she had alliences with the Windclan & Thunderclan leaders. The alliences were kept secret. That's why you hear nothing of them while reading. Her deputy, Lightningeye, was a fearsome and formidable but nonetheless, a caring, deputy. Lightningeye's apprentice, moonpaw, now known as moonpool, is a well trained warrior. She is always keeping our medicine cat, Stoneberry, stocked with herbs. In the begining, Amberclan was only two young lost kits. These two kits were Amberstar and Lightningeye. They trained one another, they were mentors and apprentences to eachother. As Amberstar says;You can never learn, without trying. That quote from Amberstar still hangs around Amberclan to this day.
